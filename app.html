Our Service

1. Medical Detoxification

A safe and supervised detox program to help individuals manage withdrawal symptoms and begin their recovery journey with medical support.

2. Inpatient Rehabilitation

A structured residential program offering therapy, counseling, and medical care for individuals recovering from substance abuse or mental health issues.

3. Outpatient Programs

Flexible treatment plans for those who need support while maintaining their daily responsibilities, including therapy sessions and group counseling.

4. Counseling & Behavioral Therapy

One-on-one and group therapy sessions, including cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) and trauma-informed care, to promote emotional healing and long-term recovery.

5. Family Support & Education

Workshops and counseling for family members to help them understand addiction and mental health challenges while providing the necessary support for their loved ones.
